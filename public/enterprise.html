Frequently Asked Questions

How does Orreryx collect geopolitical intelligence? +
Orreryx aggregates data from 200+ global news sources, official government statements, UN reports, and specialised conflict monitoring databases. Our AI layer classifies and scores every event within minutes of publication. Risk scores are updated every 5 minutes.
Can I integrate Orreryx data into my own systems? +
Yes. All enterprise plans include REST API access. The API returns conflict data, country risk scores, commodity price signals, and event feeds in JSON format. We also support Webhook push notifications for real-time event alerts to your own systems.
How does Orreryx compare to Control Risks, Oxford Analytica, or S&P's PRSM? +
Traditional political risk firms provide periodic reports written by analysts — typically weekly or monthly. Orreryx provides real-time continuous monitoring with quantified risk scores. We are faster and more affordable. We complement rather than replace deep-dive country analysis from specialist firms.
Is there a minimum contract term? +
Professional and Business plans are monthly with no minimum term. Enterprise plans typically involve annual agreements with custom SLAs. All plans start with a 14-day free trial.
What regions does Orreryx cover? +
Orreryx provides coverage for all 180+ UN-recognised countries with risk scoring, and deep-dive tracking for 56 active conflict zones across every region: Europe, Middle East, Asia, Africa, and the Americas.
