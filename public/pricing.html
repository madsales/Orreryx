Command is the only plan with the interactive geopolitical risk map — 13 live conflict zones with pulsing markers, market impact per zone (oil, gold, defence, FX), and country risk profiles linked to full analysis. Plus crypto war scores, CSV export, 5Y chart history, and public API access. Stratfor charges $340+/month for less. Bloomberg Terminal is $2,400/month. Orreryx Command is $34.99.
Can I cancel at any time? ▾
Yes. Cancel any time from your PayPal subscription settings with one click. No cancellation fees, no questions asked. You keep access until the end of the billing period.
How is payment processed? ▾
All payments are processed securely by PayPal. You can pay with PayPal balance, Visa, Mastercard, or debit card. Orreryx never stores or sees your card details.
Is there a team or enterprise plan? ▾
Yes. Enterprise plans for teams of 5+ include custom country watchlists, dedicated briefing calls, Slack integration, and priority support. Contact us via the Enterprise page for pricing.
What is the Public API? ▾
Command subscribers get access to the Orreryx Public API at /api/v1/risks — live conflict data, country risk scores, and market impact signals in JSON. Embed risk data in your own dashboards, spreadsheets, or apps.
