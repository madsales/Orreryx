Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Data we collect

We collect only your email address (for sign-in) and your subscription plan. We do not collect browsing data, financial information, or personal identifiers beyond what is required for payment.

How we use your data

Your email is used solely for account access and transactional emails (sign-in links, receipts, and service notices). We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their own marketing.

Cookies & analytics

We use cookies for analytics and advertising measurement (Google). On your first visit you are asked to accept or decline non-essential cookies, and analytics/advertising storage stays disabled until you consent (Google Consent Mode). You can change your choice at any time by clearing your browser storage for this site.

Third parties

Payments are handled by PayPal — we never see or store card details. AI processing uses Anthropic's API; queries are not retained beyond the current session. Market data is provided by third-party market-data vendors. Each processes data under its own privacy terms.

Data storage & retention

Session data is stored in your browser's localStorage. Payment records are stored in encrypted infrastructure and retained for up to 400 days for accounting and dispute purposes.

Your rights

If you are in the EU, UK, or a similar jurisdiction you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict its processing. Request account and data deletion at any time by emailing orrerysupport@gmail.com; we will respond within 30 days.
