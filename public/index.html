FAQ

Common questions

Are prices live or simulated?
Starter uses GBM simulation — realistic war-calibrated price behavior, not live exchange data. Analyst and Command connect to Twelve Data for real prices via WebSocket on 36 symbols. Analyst gets 7 live days/month; Command is unlimited.
What AI powers the briefings?
Claude by Anthropic — the same model behind Claude.ai. It receives each live news event with full geopolitical context and current market prices, then generates scenario analysis and market impact in seconds.
Can AI predict stock prices?
No. AI provides scenario-based analysis and market context — not guaranteed predictions or financial advice. All content is for informational purposes only. Always consult a licensed financial adviser before making investment decisions.
Which stocks are in the War Stocks panel?
18 equities across 5 sectors: defense (LMT, NOC, RTX, GD, BA), energy (XOM, CVX, OXY, SLB), airlines (DAL, UAL, AAL, LUV), shipping (ZIM, Maersk, FedEx), and safe haven (GLD, SLV). Each shows price, % change from pre-war baseline, and reason.
What payment methods are accepted?
All payments via PayPal — Visa, Mastercard, debit cards, and PayPal balance. We never see or store card details.

Yes. Cancel from your Account page at any time. You keep full access until the end of your current billing period. No cancellation fees, no questions asked.
