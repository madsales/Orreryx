FAQ

Common questions

Are prices live or simulated?
Starter plan uses GBM simulation — realistic war-calibrated price behavior, not live exchange data. Analyst and Command plans connect to Twelve Data for real exchange prices via WebSocket streaming on 36 symbols. Analyst gets 7 live days per month; Command is unlimited.
What AI powers the briefings?
Claude by Anthropic — the same model behind Claude.ai. It receives the latest war event and full geopolitical context (Hormuz, DEFCON status, current prices) then generates specific market impact analysis in seconds.
Which stocks are tracked in War Stocks?
18 individual equities across 5 sectors: defense (LMT, NOC, RTX, GD, BA), energy (XOM, CVX, OXY, SLB), airlines (DAL, UAL, AAL, LUV), shipping (ZIM, Maersk, FedEx), and safe haven (GLD, SLV). Each shows price, % change from pre-war baseline, and war reason.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, cancel anytime from your account dashboard. You keep access until end of the billing period. No fees, no questions.
What's the difference between the three plans?
Starter ($0.99) gives the full platform with AI briefings and simulation data. Analyst ($14.99) adds live Twelve Data exchange feed for 7 days/month — real prices, WebSocket streaming. Command ($34.99) gives unlimited live data with no monthly cutoff, plus live crypto market (12 assets), portfolio alerts, watchlist, 30-day history, and email digest.
Is my payment safe?
All payments are processed by Stripe — the infrastructure used by Amazon, Google, and Shopify. We never see or store your card details.
