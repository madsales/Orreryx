# OrreryX — Content Strategy Skills

> Framework for all content creation: what to make, why, and for whom.

---

## Content Pillars

All OrreryX content maps to one of these five pillars:

### 1. Signal (Informational)
Live geopolitical events and their market impact. The core product value.
- "Iran strikes → oil futures up 2.1% → here's what else moves"
- War stocks tracker updates
- Conflict zone briefings

### 2. Education (How-To)
Teaching users how geopolitics connects to markets.
- "How sanctions move currency markets"
- "What the Taiwan Strait risk premium means for semiconductors"
- "Safe-haven assets: when gold rises, when it doesn't"

### 3. Proof (Credibility)
Showing OrreryX was right, ahead of the market.
- "Last week we flagged X. Here's what happened."
- Comparison: "Bloomberg covered this 3 days after OrreryX"
- User outcomes (if available)

### 4. Community (Engagement)
Drawing people in through discussion and participation.
- "What's your read on India-Pakistan this week?" (poll)
- Reddit: genuine analysis in r/geopolitics, r/investing
- Discord: daily briefings, discussion prompts

### 5. Product (Conversion)
Showing what OrreryX does with a CTA.
- Feature spotlights ("Email alerts just got faster")
- "Here's what Analyst tier shows you right now"
- Pricing/upgrade prompts

---

## Content Ratio

For ongoing social output:
- 50% Signal (informational, no direct CTA)
- 25% Education
- 15% Proof
- 10% Product (conversion-focused)

Never run 3+ product posts in a row. It feels like ads.

## Evergreen vs. Timely Content

**Timely (prioritize):**
- Breaking events → publish within 2 hours
- Weekly market preview → Monday morning
- Post-event analysis → same day

**Evergreen (batch in advance):**
- "How geopolitics moves oil" explainer
- "War stocks 101" guide
- Competitor comparison posts
- Feature tour posts

Maintain a buffer of 7+ evergreen posts ready to publish when news is slow.

## SEO-Adjacent Content Angles

High-intent search queries to target with blog/landing page content:
- "geopolitical risk market impact"
- "how wars affect oil prices"
- "Ukraine Russia stock market impact"
- "Taiwan strait semiconductor stocks"
- "Iran sanctions oil price"
- "Bloomberg alternative for retail investors"
- "geopolitical intelligence platform"

Each post should naturally answer one of these queries.

---

## Content Quality Filters

Before publishing, ask:
1. Does this provide signal (specific insight) or just noise (generic comment)?
2. Would a retail investor find this actionable?
3. Is there a specific asset, ticker, or price movement mentioned?
4. Does this fit one of the 5 pillars?
5. Is there a clear CTA or is it pure value?

**If the answer to #1 and #2 are both "no", don't publish.**

## Topics Always Worth Covering

- Any escalation in: Ukraine-Russia, Iran, Taiwan Strait, India-Pakistan, Gaza, North Korea
- Oil futures moves > 1.5%
- Gold safe-haven rallies
- Defense stock movements (LMT, RTX, NOC, BAE)
- Grain/wheat supply disruptions
- Shipping lane disruptions (Strait of Hormuz, Red Sea, South China Sea)
- Sanctions announcements
- Nuclear risk escalations
