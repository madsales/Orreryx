## Google Responsive Search Ads (RSA)

### Specs
- 15 headlines (max 30 characters each)
- 4 descriptions (max 90 characters each)
- Google mixes and matches — write for any combination

### Headline Angles (write 3 per angle)
1. **Price contrast** — "Bloomberg at $2000. We're $14.99"
2. **Speed/edge** — "Know Before Markets Move"
3. **Feature specificity** — "13 Live Conflict Zones Tracked"
4. **Problem** — "Missing Geopolitical Signals?"
5. **Free trial** — "Try Free — No Credit Card"

### Description Angles
1. Contrast: "Hedge funds pay thousands for geopolitical data. OrreryX gives retail investors the same signal, live."
2. Value: "Real-time conflict data → direct market impact. Oil, gold, defense, crypto. From $0.99/mo."
3. Urgency: "By the time Bloomberg covers it, the trade has moved. OrreryX puts you ahead."
4. Free trial: "3-day free trial. Analyst-tier access. No credit card required. Start now."

### Keywords to Target
- "geopolitical risk investing"
- "bloomberg alternative"
- "war stocks tracker"
- "geopolitical intelligence platform"
- "conflict market impact"
- "real time geopolitical data"

## LinkedIn Ads

### Specs
- Intro text: 150 characters (visible before "see more")
- Headline: 70 characters
- Description: 100 characters
- Image: 1200×627px recommended

### Audience Targeting
- Job titles: Portfolio Manager, Equity Analyst, Trader, Financial Advisor, Hedge Fund Analyst
- Industries: Financial Services, Investment Management
- Skills: Bloomberg Terminal, Financial Analysis, Portfolio Management

### Angles
1. **Professional credibility:** "The geopolitical briefing your Bloomberg terminal skips."
2. **Cost comparison:** "Your Bloomberg subscription: $2,000/month. OrreryX: $34.99. Both track geopolitical risk."
3. **Speed:** "Our analysts see it before Reuters publishes. Now you can too."

### LinkedIn Ad Copy Template
```
Intro: [Problem or contrast in ≤150 chars]
Headline: [Benefit or CTA in ≤70 chars]
CTA Button: "Try Free" or "Learn More"
```

## Meta (Facebook/Instagram) Ads

### Specs
- Primary text: 125 characters (before truncation)
- Headline: 40 characters
- Description: 30 characters
- Image: 1080×1080px (square) or 1080×1350px (portrait)

### Audience Targeting
- Interest: Investing, Bloomberg, Reuters, Geopolitics, Forex, Commodities
- Age: 28–55
- Behavior: Frequent travelers, finance app users
- Lookalike: 1–2% lookalike of trial signups

### Angles
1. **Curiosity:** "Most investors saw oil spike. Few knew why. OrreryX shows both."
2. **Contrast:** "Bloomberg: $2,000/month. OrreryX: $14.99. Same geopolitical signal."
3. **Fear of missing out:** "Troop movements affect your portfolio. Are you watching?"

### Creative Direction
- Static image: OrreryX risk map screenshot with price overlay
- Carousel: 5 conflict zones → 5 affected assets
- Video (if available): 15-second "event → market move" demonstration

## Universal Ad Rules

1. **Lead with the hook** — first 5 words must earn attention
2. **One message per ad** — don't mix multiple benefits
3. **Specific > vague** — "$14.99/month" beats "affordable"
4. **Test 3+ variants** per campaign before scaling
5. **Never run same creative > 14 days** without refresh (ad fatigue)
6. **Track:** CTR, CPC, trial start rate, trial → paid rate
