# OrreryX — Video Marketing Skills

> How to create, distribute, and optimize video content for OrreryX.

---

## Video Content Strategy

Video is the highest-engagement format for showing the OrreryX product in action. Most effective for:
- Product demos (show, don't tell)
- Explainer content ("how geopolitics moves oil")
- Thought leadership (founder commentary on live events)

---

## Video Content Types

### 1. Product Demo (Priority 1)
**Length:** 60–90 seconds
**Goal:** Show the aha moment — event → market impact — as fast as possible
**Script structure:**
1. Hook (0–5s): "Most investors saw oil spike. Here's what OrreryX showed 4 hours earlier."
2. Screen demo (5–60s): Live dashboard, event detail, market impact panel
3. CTA (60–90s): "Free trial at orreryx.io — no credit card"

**Platform:** YouTube (SEO), Twitter/X (native video), LinkedIn (native video), Instagram Reels

### 2. "Event Breakdown" (Priority 2)
**Length:** 30–60 seconds
**Goal:** Explain a live geopolitical event and its market implications
**Cadence:** 2–3 per week (when major events occur)
**Format:** Screen recording of OrreryX dashboard + voiceover OR talking head

**Script template:**
```
[HOOK]: "[Event] just happened. Here's what moves."
[BODY]: "This typically affects [asset 1], [asset 2], and [asset 3] because..."
[CTA]: "I track this daily on OrreryX — link in bio."
```

### 3. "War Stocks" Series (Priority 3)
**Length:** 45–90 seconds
**Goal:** Spotlight one defense/energy stock per episode
**Cadence:** Weekly
**Format:** Chart analysis + geopolitical context

### 4. Tutorial (Priority 4)
**Length:** 2–5 minutes
**Goal:** Show new users how to use OrreryX
**Topics:**
- "Setting up your first email alert"
- "Reading the geopolitical risk map"
- "How to use AI event analysis"
- "War stocks tracker walkthrough"

## Platform-Specific Rules

### YouTube
- Title: Include target keyword ("geopolitical risk investing", "how war affects oil")
- Description: First 2 lines must hook (show before "more" truncation)
- Thumbnail: Face + text + OrreryX dashboard visible
- Tags: geopolitics, investing, oil, war stocks, OrreryX, bloomberg alternative
- End screen: Link to orreryx.io/app

### Twitter/X
- Native video (don't link to YouTube)
- Max 60 seconds (shorter performs better)
- Add captions — most watch without sound
- First frame must be a hook image (not a blank screen)

### Instagram Reels
- 15–30 seconds (optimal for Reels algorithm)
- Vertical format (9:16)
- Bold first frame text
- Hook in first 2 seconds
- Music/sound optional but recommended

### LinkedIn
- Native video only (links suppressed in feed)
- 1–3 minutes (longer performs better on LinkedIn than Twitter)
- Add captions (many watch muted at work)
- Professional angle: "What analysts need to know about..."

## Video Production (Lean Setup)

For screen recording demos:
- **Tool:** Loom, OBS, or QuickTime
- **Resolution:** 1080p minimum
- **Audio:** External mic (not laptop mic) if voiceover
- **Dashboard state:** Real events, not empty state

For talking head:
- Good lighting (window light or ring light)
- Clean background (or branded OrreryX background)
- 1080p webcam or phone camera in good light
- Lapel mic recommended

---

## Video SEO

For YouTube videos targeting geopolitical/investing queries:
- Title: "[Keyword]: [OrreryX Angle]"
  Example: "Ukraine Russia Oil Price: What OrreryX Tracked Before Reuters"
- Description: First 150 chars = keyword-rich summary + link to orreryx.io
- Chapters: Add timestamps for videos >3 minutes
- Subtitles: Upload SRT file for every video

---

## Repurposing Videos

One video → multiple assets:
- YouTube full cut → Twitter 60-second clip → Instagram 30-second Reel
- Extract audio quote → static social image with quote
- Transcript → LinkedIn post or blog post
- 5 short clips → YouTube Shorts series

Maximize distribution by repurposing, not creating from scratch each time.
