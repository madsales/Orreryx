# OrreryX — Co-Marketing Skills

> How to partner with other brands, newsletters, and platforms to grow OrreryX.

---

## What Co-Marketing Means for OrreryX

Co-marketing = partnering with complementary brands to reach each other's audiences.

**OrreryX's position in partnerships:** We provide geopolitical intelligence that adds value to finance, trading, and news audiences. Partners get unique content; we get audience access.

---

## Ideal Partner Profile

**Best partners share:**
- Retail investor / trader / analyst audience
- Interest in global macro, commodities, or geopolitics
- Non-competitive positioning (they don't do what OrreryX does)

**Examples:**
- Finance newsletters (Finimize, The Daily Upside, Morning Brew Money, Sherwood News)
- Trading platforms (Webull, eToro, tastytrade — if they'll partner)
- Macro-focused podcasts
- Personal finance influencers with macro/investing angle
- Tools: charting platforms (TradingView community), screeners (Finviz)

---

## Partnership Types

### 1. Newsletter Swap
**What:** Each party promotes the other in their newsletter
**Best for:** Finance/macro newsletters with 5k+ subscribers
**Pitch:** "We'll feature [Partner] to our [X] subscribers. In return, a mention to yours."
**Our send:** One email or one section in an existing email
**CTA for partner to send:** "Free trial at orreryx.io/app — geopolitical risk for investors"

### 2. Guest Content
**What:** OrreryX writes a conflict-to-market analysis for a partner's newsletter/blog
**Best for:** Finance newsletters that publish market analysis
**Format:** 300-word analysis of a live conflict's market impact, bylined "via OrreryX"
**Include:** Natural CTA at end: "Full analysis and live data at orreryx.io/app"

### 3. Joint Webinar / Twitter Space
**What:** OrreryX + partner host a live discussion on a geopolitical event and market impact
**Best for:** Finance YouTubers, Twitter Spaces hosts with >5k followers
**Format:** 30–45 minutes, live Q&A
**CTA:** Both parties promote to their audiences beforehand + follow-up email

### 4. Affiliate / Referral Agreement
**What:** Partner earns commission per paying OrreryX subscriber they refer
**Commission:** 20–30% of first payment
**Tracking:** Unique referral URL with UTM parameter (`?ref=partnerslug`)
**Best for:** Finance bloggers, newsletter writers with engaged small audiences

### 5. Data / Tool Integration
**What:** OrreryX provides a widget or data feed to a partner's product
**Best for:** Fintech apps, trading platforms, news aggregators
**Example:** "Powered by OrreryX" conflict risk widget embedded in a portfolio tracker
**Value exchange:** Brand exposure + backlink

---

## Partner Evaluation Criteria

Before committing to a partnership:
- Audience size: 2k+ subscribers or equivalent
- Audience quality: finance/investing/macro readers (not general public)
- Engagement rate: >20% open rate for newsletters, >2% engagement for social
- Non-competitive: they don't offer geopolitical market intelligence
- Brand alignment: professional, data-driven, not conspiratorial

**Red flags:** Low engagement rates, political bias, unverified audience claims

---

## Tracking Partnership Performance

For each partnership, track:
- Signups attributed (UTM `?ref=partnerslug`)
- Trial → paid rate for partnership cohort
- Revenue generated

**Good partnership:** >50 signups, >10% trial → paid
**Poor partnership:** <10 signups, review and don't repeat

---

## Partnership Terms (Standard)

- Duration: 1 newsletter feature or 1 content piece (no long-term commitment for first test)
- Content rights: Each party owns their own content; can cross-post with attribution
- No exclusivity for newsletter swaps
- Affiliate agreements: 30-day cookie, monthly payment, minimum $50 threshold
