# OrreryX — Cold Email Skills

> How to write and send cold outreach emails for OrreryX.

---

## Cold Email Principles

1. **2–4 word subject line, lowercase, no punctuation**
   - `markets moved today`
   - `quick question`
   - `oil and geopolitics`
   - `are you watching this`

2. **Lead with a specific observation, not a pitch**
   - Bad: "Hi, I'm from OrreryX and we help investors..."
   - Good: "Oil dropped 2.3% last Thursday. Most investors didn't know why until after the trade."

3. **One CTA. Not two.**
   - Either: "Worth a look?" (soft)
   - Or: "Try it free at orreryx.io/app" (direct)
   - Never both.

4. **Keep it under 100 words**
   - Longer = lower reply rate
   - If you can't say it in 100 words, you don't know what you're selling

5. **Personalize the first line**
   - Reference their work, newsletter, fund, or recent post
   - Generic openers get ignored

## Follow-Up Sequence

3 touches max. Tone escalates gently.

| Touch | Day | Tone |
|-------|-----|------|
| 1 | Day 0 | Curious, low-pressure |
| 2 | Day 4 | Brief, reference email 1 |
| 3 | Day 10 | Short close: "still relevant?" |

**Day 4 follow-up:**
```
Hey [Name], just following up. Didn't want to assume it wasn't 
useful — let me know if you want to talk. Otherwise I won't bother 
you again.
```

**Day 10 close:**
```
Last note — still building OrreryX. If timing's off, no worries. 
Free trial still at orreryx.io/app if you ever want to check it out.
```

## Who to Target

**Best cold email targets:**
- Independent research analysts (Substack, Seeking Alpha)
- Hedge fund analysts at boutique funds (< 20 people)
- Financial journalists covering geopolitics + markets
- Twitter/X finance influencers (>5k followers, geopolitics angle)
- CFAs working at family offices

**Where to find them:**
- LinkedIn: "Financial Analyst" + "geopolitics" or "commodities" in bio
- Twitter/X: follow r/geopolitics community accounts, look at who they interact with
- Substack: search for finance + geopolitics newsletters

## What NOT to Do in Cold Email

- Don't attach anything — it triggers spam filters
- Don't use HTML templates — plain text outperforms
- Don't use "I" in the first sentence (LinkedIn tip applies here too)
- Don't CC multiple people on the same email
- Don't send from a free Gmail — use noreply@orreryx.io or founder@orreryx.io
- Don't send more than 50 cold emails/day from one domain (deliverability risk)
