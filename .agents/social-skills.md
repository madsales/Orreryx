# OrreryX — Social Media Skills

> Reference when writing or generating social content for any platform.

---

## Core Structure: Hook-First

Every post leads with the consequence, not the event.

**Formula:** [What just happened] → [What moves as a result] → [Why you need to know]

❌ "Tensions rise in Taiwan Strait"
✅ "TSMC suppliers are pricing in a 40% supply disruption. Here's what moves."

---

## Twitter/X

**Goal:** Stop the scroll. One sharp insight. Drive to orreryx.io/app.

**Rules:**
- First line = the hook. Rewrite it 3x, keep the sharpest version.
- Never open with "🚨" or "BREAKING" — that's noise, not signal
- Lead with the market consequence, not the geopolitical summary
- Include 1–2 specific tickers, assets, or commodities
- Max 1 emoji if needed. Zero is fine.
- CTA: "Track it live → orreryx.io/app" or "Full picture at orreryx.io/app"
- Threads: Hook tweet → 3 signal tweets → 1 context tweet → CTA tweet

**Engagement formats that work:**
- "Most people missed this" + the thing they missed
- "Oil is up 2.1%. The reason is ___" (fill the blank)
- Prediction with timeframe: "Watch gold this week. Here's why."
- Contrast: "Bloomberg: [event]. What actually moves: [asset]."

---

## LinkedIn

**Goal:** Professional credibility. Show analytical depth. Secondary: drive analyst-tier signups.

**Rules:**
- Open with a data point or sharp observation, not a headline
- 3–5 short paragraphs. First paragraph must hook.
- Explain the mechanism: why does this event move this asset?
- Close with a question or implication for professionals
- CTA is subtle: "We track this at OrreryX" not "SIGN UP NOW"
- No hashtag spam — 2–3 max, only if genuinely relevant

**Tone:** Senior analyst briefing a colleague. Calm. Specific. No panic.

---

## Instagram

**Goal:** New audience discovery. Visual-first. Brand awareness over direct conversion.

**Rules:**
- Caption opens with a dramatic, specific opener (not vague)
- Tell a micro-story: setup → tension → resolution/implication
- Use line breaks aggressively for readability
- 3–5 relevant hashtags at the bottom
- Visuals: risk map screenshots, asset price charts, conflict zone graphics

---

## Banned Phrases

- "cutting-edge", "paradigm shift", "synergy", "disruptive"
- "EVERYTHING IS BLOWING UP"
- "comprehensive", "robust", "leverage" (as a verb)
- Starting with "Exciting news"
- "Check out our platform"

---

## One CTA Per Post

Never mix "sign up" + "learn more" + "upgrade" in the same post. Pick one.

Default CTAs by platform:
- Twitter: `orreryx.io/app`
- LinkedIn: "OrreryX tracks this in real time"
- Instagram: "Link in bio → OrreryX"
