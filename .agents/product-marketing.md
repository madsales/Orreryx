## 4. Core Pain Points We Solve

1. **Speed gap** — Major geopolitical events move markets before mainstream media catches up. OrreryX closes that gap.
2. **Translation gap** — Smart people follow the news but don't know which stocks, commodities, or currencies are affected. We translate events into market language.
3. **Cost gap** — Hedge funds pay $349–$500/month for inferior, weekly data. Retail investors have had no comparable alternative. Until now.
4. **Noise gap** — Financial news is full of earnings talk, inflation data, Fed commentary. Geopolitical risk — which often drives everything else — is buried or absent.

## 10. Brand Voice

**Tone:** Direct, intelligent, calm under pressure. Like a trusted analyst who never panics.

**Not:** Hype-heavy, salesy, breathless. Not "EVERYTHING IS BLOWING UP" — that's Twitter.

**Formula:** Factual observation → why it matters → what moves → calm confidence

**Examples:**

✅ "Iran launched strikes on 3 bases. Oil futures are up 2.1%. Here's what else moves."  
❌ "BREAKING: GLOBAL CHAOS — Markets in TURMOIL!!!"

✅ "Most investors are watching earnings this week. We're watching the Strait of Hormuz."  
❌ "Our cutting-edge platform leverages AI to provide comprehensive geopolitical insights."

**For social posts:** Lead with the hook. One clear insight per post. CTA is "orreryx.io/app" — always present but never pushy.

**For emails:** Human voice. Short sentences. Write like a person, not a newsletter template.
