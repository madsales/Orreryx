# OrreryX — Pricing Skills

> How to present, frame, and optimize OrreryX pricing for maximum conversion.

---

## Current Pricing Tiers

| Tier | Price | Key Features |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Starter | $0.99/month | Basic conflict data, 15-min delayed, 1 AI analysis/day |
| Analyst | $14.99/month | Real-time data, email alerts, interactive risk map, 10 AI analyses/day |
| Command | $34.99/month | Everything + 3D globe, unlimited AI, exports, priority support |
| Free Trial | 3 days | Analyst-level access, no credit card |

---

## Pricing Psychology in Practice

### Anchor High, Reveal Low
Always introduce Bloomberg first:
> "Bloomberg Terminal: $2,000+/month. OrreryX: from $0.99."

Never lead with your own price — lead with the anchor.

### Make Analyst the Obvious Choice
- Starter should feel limited (it is — delayed data, minimal AI)
- Command should feel premium but optional
- **Analyst ($14.99) is the sweet spot** — name it "Most Popular" or "Best Value"

### Frame per day, not per month
> "$14.99/month = $0.50/day. Less than your morning coffee."

### Annual pricing (if/when offered)
- Annual = 2 months free (10 × monthly price)
- "Pay $149/year instead of $179.88"
- Position as: "the choice serious investors make"

## Pricing Page Copy

**Hero:**
> "Geopolitical intelligence for investors. Not $2,000/month."

**Comparison table must include:**
- OrreryX tiers
- Bloomberg (as the scary premium anchor)
- Free news (as the weak alternative)

**Below pricing:**
> "Free trial includes Analyst-level access. No credit card. Cancel anytime."

## Objection Handling at Pricing

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Too expensive" | "Bloomberg is $2,000/month. We're $0.50/day." |
| "I'll use free news" | "Free news doesn't show which assets move from troop movements." |
| "I'll try the free tier" | "Free tier is permanent. Analyst trial is 3 days — worth trying." |
| "Do I need Command?" | "Most investors get everything they need from Analyst." |

## Upgrade Triggers (When to Show Upgrade Prompts)

Show Analyst upgrade prompt when free/starter user:
- Hits 3 AI analysis limit in a day
- Tries to access real-time data (sees 15-min delay notice)
- Tries to enable email alerts
- Has been on platform 7+ days (day-7 email)
- Reads 5+ events in one session

**Prompt tone:** Helpful, not pushy. "Want real-time? Analyst unlocks it."

---

## Discount Strategy

**Approved discounts:**
- `COMEBACK20` — 20% off, for churned users only
- Referral reward — 1 free month per 3 referrals
- Pause offer — 30 days free pause for at-risk users

**Never offer:**
- Blanket sitewide discounts (devalues brand)
- Discounts to new users who haven't tried free tier
- Stacking discounts

**When to offer COMEBACK20:**
- Only in dunning D+10 "last chance" email
- Only to users who had paid at least once
- Never proactively on pricing page

## Revenue Operations Notes

- Trial → paid conversion target: 15%+
- Analyst tier is primary revenue driver
- Starter ($0.99) exists to reduce barrier to entry, not as main revenue
- Command tier exists to anchor Analyst and serve power users
- Track: trial start rate, trial → paid rate, MRR, churn rate
